To use just put in your flashcards in the textbox on the left!

The format should be the question, three hyphens ('-') and then the answer. Then just put in a blank line and repeat for as many flashcards as you need!

If you want to put in a table, put '!table' on a line on it's own and below that put in your table using any standard tab or space seperated format. Use `!endtable` to signify that the table has ended. See the example on the left.
